How Penny Saver works

Everyday, Penny Saver will deduct any pennies you have in your Venmo account balance. For example, if you have $14.34 in your Venmo account, Penny Saver will take 34 cents and put it in a Penny Saver account. These pennies start to add up!

You can always come back to this page to see your balance, or check the amount when Penny Saver deducts any pennies.

You can choose at what threshold Penny Saver will not deduct pennies. For example, if you choose $10.00, and your balance is $9.68, Penny Saver won't take the 68 cents. When your Venmo account balance goes above $10.00, Penny Saver will reactivate.

Saving more is easy! If you want to manually add money to your Penny Saver account, you can pay pennysaver from your Venmo account.

Finally, when you want your money back, you can charge pennysaver up to the total amount. You should see the money back in your Venmo account shortly. Just remember, if you don't do this step, you'll be on the way to saving for a rainy day, when you really need those pennies.
